The Beginning

In the beginning I was was introduced to Trading Card Games, specifically Pokemon. I had a great time when I was younger, playing the games, and as I grew older, I gained an interest in Computers and technology, an inevitably, Pokemon cards were no longer of interest.

About Me

Four years ago I got back into Trading Cards, with an interest in the cool art and nostalgic feel, I bought every new set that would come out, and I got back into the Trading Card Games. After a while I set out to get a couple of dollars for my cards and I realized there was a big market for cards, escpecially right after relase. so I set out to start a business to sell, and here we are today.

Our Mission

After I got back into Trading cards and saw the big market for these cards, I would buy and sell. Every new set that would come out. After buying cards from Ebay, my local card shop, and many other places, I realized just how hard it is to buy from the same place. Other services provide the ability to buy different TCG Cards, but no website sells them all from the same place, allowing anyone to list their item on their website. With this it would take a while to get what you purchasedm especially if you buy it from different places. Our mission was to be the One Stop Shop for all your TCG needs, one big order of Pokemon, Magic, Final Fantasy, and accessories all in the same delivery, and delivered on the same day!
